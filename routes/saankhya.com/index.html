ABOUT US

Saankhya Learning Centre, is an after-school for Mathematics and Science to provide the perfect blend of learning and competitive training. With a variety of academic programs, we don’t just provide a comprehensive learning of concepts but rather engage personally with each student to make them original, creative and better problem solvers. We believe in a learning process that enables children to become better aware towards the daily life and incorporate their lessons learnt towards self-engaged situations. With such a vision, we wish to mould every child, so he or she can excel in the most competitive exams of the country and make a self-fulfilling and promising career for him or herself.

Learning at Saankhya

At Saankhya, our classroom is lot more than just a teacher lecturing to a group, rather a group of enthusiasts keenly discussing on a phenomena with everyone contributing to it. The faculty moderates the sessions, imparts basic concepts and makes them practice on some challenging situations. This is in turn followed by comprehensive evaluation and regular feedback.
